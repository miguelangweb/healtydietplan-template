If we are going to bake them, preheat the oven to 180ºC and prepare the mold, with two standard cavities or four smaller ones. If it is silicone or non-stick, it will not be necessary to grease it. In a bowl, beat the whole egg with the extra egg white (we used pasteurized, to better measure the quantity), the cheese, the almonds and the herbs to taste. Keto waffles. Low-Carb Fitness Muffins


Despite the fact that I do not worry too much about the amount of carbohydrates in my diet -I do care about the quality and total balance of my diet-, from time to time I prepare recipes of the so-called more protein lowcarb, like these fitness rolls protein rich. I was inspired in this recipe using a little of the mixture of seeds and cereals that I had at home and that each one can adapt to their taste without problems. They are, of course, “fake rolls”, but they come out a kind of very fluffy and satiating buns that we can shape more chubby to open them in half, or, as in my case, flatter to combine them with each other.

  • Ingredients

  • For 8 units
  • Eggs 3
  • Low-fat smoothie fresh cheese or natural yogurt 250 g
  • Sunflower seeds 40 g
  • Flax seeds 30g
  • Ground flax seeds 10 g
  • Chia seeds 20g
  • Poppy or sesame seeds 10 g
  • Oat bran 80 g
  • Ground almond 60 g
  • Chemical yeast (propellant) 10 g
  • Salt 4g

  • How to make low carb fitness muffins

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Total time 40m
  • Elaboration 15 m
  • Cooking 25m
  • Repose 30h

Lightly beat the eggs with the queso fresco or yogurt in a medium bowl. Add all the seeds and mix. Add the bran, ground almonds, ground flax, yeast and salt. Mix gently and well until a homogeneous mass is obtained. Cover and let rest in the fridge for 30 minutes. Preheat the oven to 180ºC. Prepare a large tray or two medium ones, with baking paper. Divide the dough into 8 portions, giving a more or less rounded shape. They can be made thicker or flatter, to taste, and they can also be made inside a cupcake pan or similar. Bake for about 20-25 minutes, until golden brown. Wait a bit out of the oven before transferring to a rack. Let them cool completely before storing in an airtight container. If they are not consumed in a couple of days or if it is very hot, it is better to store them in the fridge or freeze them well wrapped.

Scrambled eggs in water.

A technique as surprising as it is effective and quick to cook an easy and fluffy scrambled egg.

  • Ingredients

  • For 2 persons
  • Fresh eggs and happy chickens 4
  • Salt to taste
  • ground black pepper (optional)

  • How to make scrambled eggs in water

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Total time 6 min
  • 5m Elaboration
  • Cooking 1m
To obtain a better result, it is advisable to crack the eggs over a fine mesh strainer so that they release the water that the white usually has; if they are very fresh we can save it. Put a medium-sized pot or casserole with plenty of water to heat at medium power, with the lid so that it takes temperature faster.
Place the eggs in a bowl, season with salt and pepper and break the yolk with a fork or chopsticks, stirring them without beating excessively so as not to introduce too much air, or do it to taste. When the water comes to a medium boil, add a couple of pinches of salt and stir the water in a spiral with a large spoon to form a whirlpool.

Nice in oil.

When this delicious fish is in full season, it is worth preparing it in homemade olive oil.
Essential to eat well in summer, served fresh from the fridge.

  • Ingredients

  • For 4 units
  • Bonito (the tail part) 1kg
  • Extra virgin olive oil 1l
  • lemon 1
  • Salt
  • Fresh rosemary branches 2

  • How to make bonito in oil, lemon and rosemary

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Total time
  • Cover a baking dish with a splash of extra virgin olive oil. Wash the tuna under a fine stream of cold water, on both sides. We do not want to damage it so we will be careful to do it gently and quickly. We dry it with absorbent paper and place it, skin down, in the dish. Generously season the tuna piece, distributing the salt over the entire surface. Generously water with extra virgin olive oil (we will use this oil later, so don't be afraid!). We cover the tuna with thin slices of lemon and previously washed and undrained rosemary leaves. Cover the bowl with aluminum foil. Roast in a pre-heated oven at 220ºC for 20 minutes. We remove the dish from the oven and let it cool before removing the skin and the central bone and blunt it. Strain the oil and distribute it among four glass jars. Fill with more extra virgin olive oil and, once cold, store in the fridge to consume within two weeks.

Baked salmon with nuts

We can skip the zucchini and simply prepare the fish with its nut coating, which, in addition to adding healthy fats and other essential nutrients, creates a very tasty crispy crust.

  • Ingredients

  • For 2 persons
  • Fresh salmon in a loin 300 gr
  • Walnuts 20gr
  • Sliced almond 20 gr
  • Pistachios 20gr
  • Extra virgin olive oil (a teaspoon) 5 ml
  • Zucchini 1

  • How to make baked salmon with nuts

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Total time 15 m
  • Elaboration 5m
  • Cooking 10m
  • Repose 5m
We start by crushing the different nuts to make a granular mixture with which we will make a crust on the salmon. To make this dish, it is convenient to use a large loin or two ingots -one per person- so I remind you that here you can see how to cut it to get the most out of this fish.
With a brush, lightly brush the salmon loin with a little extra virgin olive oil and then place it on the plate where we have the mixture of nuts. We press a little so that they stick and we pass the fish to the oven source.
In it we will have put a "bed" of courgette slices, which will later be the garnish for our baked salmon recipe, thus completing a healthy and flavorful dish. We bake after having preheated to 220º and let cook for about 10 minutes until we see that the salmon has changed color. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving so that the salmon finishes cooking with the remaining heat.

Turkey Curry

We can skip the zucchini and simply prepare the fish with its nut coating, which, in addition to adding healthy fats and other essential nutrients, creates a very tasty crispy crust.

  • Ingredients

  • For 4 persons
  • Natural yogurt 250 gr
  • Onion 80gr
  • 50gr cream cheese
  • Evaporated milk 50 ml
  • Ground curry (2 tablespoons) 15 gr
  • Fresh coriander leaves 12
  • Salt

  • How to make turkey curry in five minutes

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Total time 10 m
  • Elaboration 5m
  • Cooking 5m
We cut the turkey tenderloin in emincés, that is, in strips of approximately 1 x 4 centimeters and season. We can cut the turkey into thicker strips, but then we will need to slightly increase the cooking time so that it does not remain raw. Peel the onion and cut it into fine julienne.
In a microwave steamer, mix the plain yogurt together with the cream cheese, evaporated milk, ground curry, and chopped fresh coriander leaves. If you don't like coriander, you can do without it. If, on the other hand, you really like cilantro, then increase the amount as you like. Season and stir with a whisk until integrated.
Add the turkey and onion to the mixture in the steamer and stir well so that no pieces are left unmarinated. We cover and put it in a microwave oven, where we cook it at 800w for five minutes. If we don't have a steamer, we can use a microwave-safe container and cover it with plastic wrap, pricking it before putting it in the oven.
Once the cooking time has elapsed, remove the steamer (or the container that we use) from the oven and let it rest for five minutes, without removing the lid. While our turkey curry rests, we can set the table and have everything ready to serve freshly made, sprinkled with sesame seeds.

Baked salmon loin with ajonesa crust

Another great recipe even simpler, in case the nuts are not so to our liking. Mayonnaise goes wonderfully with fish and will delight even the biggest garlic lovers.

  • Ingredients

  • For 4 persons
  • Fresh salmon loin 800 gr
  • Mayonnaise 50 gr
  • Garlic salt
  • 5 gr

  • How to make baked salmon loin with ajonesa crust

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Total time 20 m
  • Elaboration 5m
  • Cooking 10m

Clean the salmon loin well, removing any possible bones it may have along with the skin. We can portion the loin into four pieces at this time or leave it whole and do it after baking. To taste and according to what is most comfortable for each one, although I prefer to leave it whole because it stays juicier.
If we have mayonnaise ready, we only need to mix it in a large bowl with the garlic salt. Otherwise, we will have to prepare it at the moment. To do this, we follow the instructions on how to make mayonnaise or mayonnaise sauce and we will have it ready in a few minutes.
Place the salmon on a baking sheet covered with parchment paper and cover it with the garlic sauce. Cook in the oven, pre-heated to 220ºC for about 15 minutes or until the ajonesa acquires a beautiful golden color. If we have chosen to portion the salmon loin, we will have to reduce the time slightly. We serve immediately.

Avocado Truffles

An ideal sweet and energetic snack to take advantage of the ripest avocados in the pantry.

  • Ingredients

  • For 18 units
  • Ripe avocado 1
  • Dark chocolate for desserts 80 gr
  • 10gr butter
  • Cocoa powder

  • How to make avocado truffles

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Total time 15 m
  • Elaboration 12m
  • Cooking 3m
  • Rest 45m
Melt the dark chocolate and butter in the microwave in three sets of one minute. Open the avocado and remove the bone. With the help of a spoon, extract the pulp and mash it with a fork until you get a cream.
Pour the melted chocolate over the mashed avocado pulp and work the mixture until smooth. You will get a dough with a very light texture that is impossible to handle, so it is best to put the dough in the freezer for about 45 minutes.
After that time, remove the mixture from the freezer and form the truffles by making balls with spoons or with your hands. Coat them later with pure cocoa powder to obtain their usual appearance. Store the truffles in the fridge if you are going to eat them right away, they last a couple of days in good condition.
